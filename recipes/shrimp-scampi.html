Shrimp Scampi

Description

this classic pasta dish is delicious paired with a glass of wine & a weekend in!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup of broth or dry white wine
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 lbs large shrimp, shelled
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley
  • freshly squeezed juice of 1/2 lemon
  • cooked angel hair pasta

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter with olive oil. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add wine or broth, salt, red pepper flakes and plenty of black pepper and bring to a simmer. Let wine reduce by half, about 2 minutes.
  2. Add shrimp and sauté until they just turn pink, 2 to 4 minutes depending upon their size. Stir in the parsley and lemon juice and serve over pasta.
